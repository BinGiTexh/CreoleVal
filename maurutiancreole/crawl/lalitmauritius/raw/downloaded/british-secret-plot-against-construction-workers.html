British Secret Plot against Construction Workers

08.03.2012

This though long known, was difficult to prove. We are reproducing below an article below from the excellent news site "In These Times" which brings the proof (http://inthesetimes.com/working/entry/12842/blacklisted_uk_workers_still_struggle).
