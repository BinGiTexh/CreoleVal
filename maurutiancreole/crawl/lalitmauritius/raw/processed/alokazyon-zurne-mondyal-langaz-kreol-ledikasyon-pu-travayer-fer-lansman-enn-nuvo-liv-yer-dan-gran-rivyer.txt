Alokazyon Zurne Mondyal Langaz Kreol, Ledikasyon pu travayer fer lansman enn nuvo liv yer dan Gran Rivyer
29.10.2003
. "Eski Guvernman pe tuy Bhojpuri ek kreol ?" samem tit sa liv ki LPT finn lanse yer. Liv-la baze lor diskur ki Dr.Tove Skutnabb-Kangas ti fer an Septam lane deryer dan Teat Port-Louis dan kad kanpayn Konesans pu tu Dimunn. Liv-la tret lor zenosid lingwistik dan lekol ek lor diversite langaz. Diskur lansman ti fer par Georges Cheung, redakter an sef La Vie Catholique. Devianand Narrain ek Alain Ah-Vee ti koz pu Ledikasyon pu travayer. Dany Marie ti lir enn poem lor size langaz.  Dan lasistans ti ena mamb ek sinpatizan LPT ek lezot lasosyasyon, zelev kur lir-ekrir, lektyurer liniversite Moris ek lezot amure langaz Kreol.Liv-la a-vann Rs 25.00 kopi. Pu plis detay lor Tove Skutnabb-Kangas get extre anba ki sorti depi kuvertir so liv.Born in Finland, Tove Skutnabb-Kangas now lives and works in Copenhagen, Denmark. She speaks Finnish and Swedish as mother tongues. She is Vice-President of Terralingua- Partnerships for Linguistics and Biological Diversity and a member of the International Academy of Language Law.She has ben awarded one of the two 2003 Linguapax prizes (Catalan sociolinguist Aina Moll is the other recipient) for her outstanding work in the field of linguistic diversity and multilingual education. She is and has been member of many editorial boards, and has contributed to a dozen of encyclopaedias and handbooks. Her own books include: Bilingualism or Not - The Education of Minorities; Linguistic Imperialism; Linguistic Human Rights: Overcoming Linguistic Discrimination; Multilingualism for All; Linguistic Genocide in Education - or Worldwide Diversity?. At present she works at the Department of Languages and Culture at the University of Roskilde, Denmark.
