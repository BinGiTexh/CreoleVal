Ram Seegobin writes key article on "Food Refusal: The Hunger Strike as a Means of Struggle"
11.01.2007
In response to a lot of adverse comments on recent hunger strikes, Lalit member, Ram Seegobin, wrote an article in L'Express daily newspaper at the end of last year on this difficult question. It has been widely commented. The full version of the article, which he had reduced slightly for the newspaper version, is now in the documents section of our web page. Click on documents, then on "browse" and you will find it.With the documents section of our web site up and running again, we will no longer print documents in full in the "news section" but instead use the news item, like this one, in order to refer web-visitors to the documents section.
