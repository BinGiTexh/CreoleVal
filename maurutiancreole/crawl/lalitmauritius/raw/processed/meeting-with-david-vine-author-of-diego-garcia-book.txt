Meeting with David Vine, author of Diego Garcia book
08.07.2009
Two representatives of LALIT, Alain Ah-Vee and Lindsey Collen had a meeting with David Vine, author of the book "Islands of Shame: The Secret History of the U.S. Military Base on Diego Garcia", last week. He was in Mauritius and Seychelles for the launch of his book. He presented LALIT with a copy.
