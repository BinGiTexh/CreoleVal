LALIT Regional Meetings for End of the Year
21.12.2003
The Port Louis West and Rose-Hill East Regionals are holding their end-of-year meeting and parties at Grand River North West and St. Pierre respectively on Sunday 21st December. The Curepipe South Regional is to be held on Sunday 28th December.This is a time when there is a moment to look at the political work done during the year, by the Regional and also nationally. It is also a moment when everyone brings something to eat and to drink, and then it is shared as we look to the new year.
