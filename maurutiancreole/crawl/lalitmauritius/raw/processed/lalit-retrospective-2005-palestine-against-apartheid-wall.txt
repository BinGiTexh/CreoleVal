LALIT retrospective 2005: PALESTINE AGAINST APARTHEID WALL
06.01.2006
Lalit held support actions for the Palestinian people in 2005 in the Week Against the Wall, including a talk by Ragini Kistnasamy and Alain Ah-Vee two members who have been to Palestine as part of supporting the everyday struggle of a people under army occupation, and a film show and debate for the public.
