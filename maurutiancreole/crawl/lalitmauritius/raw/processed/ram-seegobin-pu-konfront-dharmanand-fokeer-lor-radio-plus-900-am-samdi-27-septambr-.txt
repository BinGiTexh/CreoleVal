Ram Seegobin pu konfront Dharmanand Fokeer lor Radio-Plus 9.00 am Samdi 27 Septam
26.09.2003
Dime, Samdi 27 Septam, Ram Seegobin ek Dharmanand Fokeer pu dan enn program Radio Plus anime par Nawaz Noorbux a 9.00 am. Tem deba la, li aksesyon o-puvwar Paul Berenger, ki pe vinn Premye Minis le 30 Septam.
