An international balance sheet of the 'broad party' strategy
26.02.2010
We recommend LALIT website visitors an interesting article by John Percy entitled "An international balance sheet of the 'broad party' strategy" relating to the transformation of revolutionary socialist parties into anti-capitalist movement.  This will be very much the subject which would be debated at the Congress of the Fourth International which is being held in Belgium and in which are particpating two LALIT members.The article is published on the website of the Revolutionary Socialist Party of Australia and the link is http://rsp.org.au/content/international-balance-sheet-%E2%80%98broad-party%E2%80%99-strategy
