May Day Message from Focus on the Global South
05.05.2007
Focus on the Global South is a progressive international research and advocacy group with offices in Thailand, Philippines, India, and Geneva, conducting research on issues of globalisation and miltarization facing the global South and contributing to the struggles of social movements and civil society around the world.On the occasion of Labor Day, we at Focus express our heartfelt message of solidarity to Lalit and to all those around the world celebrating the achievements and continuing struggles of workers worldwide. Another world is possible- one that is just and peaceful, free from war and aggression, and where right to secure work is respected. We are with you in the global movement building it.Hope this is alright.All the best,HerbertOn 04 26, 07
