Open meeting in St Pierre
02.02.2005
Lalit held a neighbourhood meeting on the campaign for an alternative political economy in St. Pierre in a college on Sunday afternoon on 30 January. Nine people were present. Ram Seegobin was invited to co-ordinate the session. He began with a 45-minute introduction to the campaign, after which general debate ensued. Some of the topics covered during the debate were very lively.
