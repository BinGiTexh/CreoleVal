Youth Commission Meets
18.02.2008
Following the LALIT Congress, the Youth Commission has seven new recruits signed up: two from the West, one from Phoenix, two from Port Louis, one from Curepipe and one from the South. At the first meeting, held Saturday 16 February, in Port Louis, topics were chosen for future discussion, including "Communalism and racism", "the death penalty" and media .  The next meeting will be in Curepipe. Please contact Ally Hosenbokus for details if you would like to join.
