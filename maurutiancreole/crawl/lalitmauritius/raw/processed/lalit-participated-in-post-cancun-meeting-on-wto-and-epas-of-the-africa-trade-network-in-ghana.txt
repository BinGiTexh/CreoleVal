Lalit participated in post-Cancun meeting on WTO and EPA's of the Africa Trade Network in Ghana
27.12.2003
Lalit member, Ashok Subron, representated Lalit in the African Continental meeting of the Africa Trade Network. This meeting was the Annual General Assembly of the ATN and was the first continental meeting held after the collapse of the WTO in Cancun. Several people's, reasearch and trade union organisations as well as social movemet and progressive NGO's participated in this important meeting held in ACCRA, Ghana. Organisations participating in the meeting were very concerned that issues that were rejected in the WTO are now finding its way in the regional agreement such as "Economoc Partnership Agreements "EPA's", "Free Trade Agreement's - FTA's"  and the AGOA.
