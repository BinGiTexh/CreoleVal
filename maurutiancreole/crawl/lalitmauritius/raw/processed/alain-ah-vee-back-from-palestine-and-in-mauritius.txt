Alain Ah-Vee Back from Palestine and in Mauritius
17.08.2004
After his three week's in Palestine, protesting against the Israeli-State's Wall, Alain Ah-Vee, LALIT militant is back in Mauritius as from today. He is very sunburnt, from time in the open air at over 35 degrees, and feeling enriched by the experience of a lifetime, alongside the people of the villages alongside the "apartheid" wall.He will be on Radio One live Wednesday 18 August at 8.15 am for a short interview with Jacques Aristide. Remember his REPORT BACK is on Saturday, 21 August at 3.00 pm at GRNW. All welcome.
