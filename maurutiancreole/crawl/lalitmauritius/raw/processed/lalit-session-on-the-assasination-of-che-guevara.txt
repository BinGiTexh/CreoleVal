LALIT Session on the Assasination of Che Guevara
02.10.2011
In commemoration of the 44th anniversary of the assasination by the CIA of the Cuban revolutionary, Che Guevara, LALIT will be holding an educational session and debate at the Mother Earth Hall, GRNW at 2:30 pm on 8 October on the life and contribution of Che Guevara who has become an icon, while many young people do not know the story of his life, or about his ideas, or even about his assassination.
