Planned protests around Bush visit to South Africa
04.07.2003
The South African Anti-War Coalition, made up of over 300 Social Movements and other people's orgnanisations, has arranged several protest actions against the visit of George Bush in South Africa. The Anti-War Coalition previously organised marches of over 40,000 people on February 15th, which was the international day of action against the war in Iraq. Now for Bush visit protest actions will be organised in Pretoria, Johannesbourg, Durban and Cape Town.  For more information on Bush Visit in South Africa go to http://southafrica.indymedia.org/
