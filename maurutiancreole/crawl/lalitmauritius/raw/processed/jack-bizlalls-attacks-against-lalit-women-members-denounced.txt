Jack Bizlall`s attacks against LALIT women members denounced
20.05.2010
The LALIT Women`s Commission has denounced Jack Bizlall`s public verbal abuse, and his subsequent vile and anti-woman reaction to sober criticism from the LALIT party. 12 women members have signed Part I of our article which is contributing to the debate on recent verbal abuse against women in the electoral campaign by mainstream political leaders.  The article is on page 27 of today`s edition of L`Express ID (20 May, 2010). The second and final part is due in L`Express ID on Monday. Should our web visitors want to consult the full text of Jack Bizlall`s verbal violence against women, please click on the "Documents" section. and click the item "Jack Bizlall Tirades against LALIT".
