The Court has also extended to 1 March 2018 the time-limit for the filing of all written statements, in accordance with Article 66, paragraph 2, of its Statute, and to 15 May 2018 the time-limit within which States and organizations having presented written statements may submit written comments on the other written statements, in accordance with Article 66, paragraph 4, of the Statute.
