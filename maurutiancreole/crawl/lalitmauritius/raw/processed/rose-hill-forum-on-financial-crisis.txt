Rose-Hill Forum on Financial Crisis
10.11.2008
Friday 7 November from 5 to 7 pm there was a LALIT forum on the Financial and Economic Crisis held at the Emile Duvivier Centre, Plaisance, Rose-Hill. Present were trade union members, a group of women who work in the Cyber-City, and LALIT branch members from Plaisance, Stanley and Camp Le Vieux. Alain Ah-Vee presided and Ram Seegobin spoke, before debate was opened to the floor.
