Kot plan ranplasman lamyant?
17.08.2020
Mardi dernye, LALIT finn avoy enn let Minis Lozman Obeegadoo pu dimann li kot plan pu ranplas lakaz lamyant? Nu finn anmemtan avoy li demand LALIT lor problem lozman dan kontext kriz koronaviris ek enn dosye lor kestyon lozman. Nu pe pibliye enn versyon kreol let ki nu finn avoye.
