New LALIT publication on Gaza Freedom March
28.01.2010
LALIT has brought out a "Palestine Bulletin: Gaza Freedom March" last week. It traces the events around the Gaza Freedom March, and analyses the political context in the Middle East in which the march was attempted. It is a bilingual (Kreol and English) puclication of 24 pages in the A4 format.Anyone who has not yet received one through a LALIT member or in the post, please contact us to get one.
