LALIT's Best Loser Book now On-Line
18.05.2010
LALIT has pleasure in informing readers that the book "Against Communalism of the Best Loser System: A Bilingual Program" is now on line. (Click on "Documents" section on the left, or above.) This 232 page book, published in 2005, is now out-of-print. However, the subject of the replacement of the Communal Best Loser System in the context of electoral reform, is imperative, so you will be able to consult its texts on line.
