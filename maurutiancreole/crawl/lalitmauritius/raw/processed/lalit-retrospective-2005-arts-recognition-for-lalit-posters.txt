LALIT retrospective 2005: ARTS: Recognition for Lalit posters
01.01.2006
The British Council exhibition and book "Up Front and Personal" of political graphics included a record 10 posters produced by Lalit, or created by Lalit members. They included posters on AIDS (the first public lecture in Mauritius, before the first case, was organized by Ledikasyon pu Travayer), on "What future is there in sugar?" from as early as 1983, women's demands for freedom to choose divorce, contraception, abortion; an anti-apartheid poster; and two on the Kreol language.
