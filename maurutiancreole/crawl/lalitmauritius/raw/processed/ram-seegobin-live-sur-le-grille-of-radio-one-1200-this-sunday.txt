Ram Seegobin live "sur le grille" of Radio One, 12.00 this Sunday
24.04.2004
Ram Seegobin, spokesperson of Lalit, will be live on Radio One, in its programme "sur le grille" as from noon this Sunday. He will be interviewed by the journalist on the prevailing political situation and fraud and corruption. Sur le Grille is the main weekly political programme of Radio One.
