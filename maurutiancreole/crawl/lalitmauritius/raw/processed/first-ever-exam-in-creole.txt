First ever exam in Creole
30.06.2008
Lalit has the pleasure in publishing the total content of this historically important document: the first-ever examination in written Creole in Mauritius. It is the 2008 final examination paper, a bilingual one, for the first-ever University of Mauritius, Faculty of Social Studies & Humanities "stand-alone" module on the Kreol language, entitled "Introduction to Creole languages". 16 students took the examination. They could choose either to reply in English or Creole. Most opted for replying in Creole. View a copy in Downloads -- photos
