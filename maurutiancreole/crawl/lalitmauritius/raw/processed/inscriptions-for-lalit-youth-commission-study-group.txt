Inscriptions for Lalit Youth Commission Study Group
30.07.2007
Young people who want to participate in a one-off study group on Karl Marx's "Critique of the Gotha Program" are requested to contact the Lalit Youth Commission. This short document interest us because, after studying Marx and Engels "Manifesto" now translated into Kreol, we learnt that this is a deepening of thoughts and a sharpening of ideas on the manifesto.Anyone wanting to attend, please contact us on 208 2132.If anyone wants to buy a copy of the Manifesto (in print form or on audio CD) in Kreol, please contact us or come around to our offices.
