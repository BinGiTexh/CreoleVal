Ashok Subron, in live debate on Radio Plus, 11.00 a.m, this Sunday
24.04.2004
Ashok Subron, spokesperson of Lalit, will also be live, this time on Radio Plus, at 11.00 a.m, this Sunday. He will be the guest, together with Mr. Rajah Badhain, of journalist Jimmy Jean Louis. The topic of the programme will be "Moralite ek Dakadans Sosyal" The Sunday debate of Radio Plus is the main political programme of Radio Plus.
