Lalit and unions to protest against the WTO's Director visit to Mauritius
07.03.2004
Lalit and all the trade union's Federation of Mauritius in separate public stands in the press announced their total objection to the decision of the government to invite the WTO Director, as chief guest to mark the 36th anniversary of Mauritian independence. Union's will be meeting this Monday to plan actions to protest against this decision. An Open Protest Letter address to the Prime Minister from Lalit is being finalised to be circulated this week.  Lalit militants will join in union's protest movement on this issue.
