International Conference against Military Bases
31.08.2005
Last week, Lalit member, Lindsey Collen participated in an international conference call to discuss the first world conference to get all military bases closed down. LALIT is participating in the organization of a world conference of the NO U.S. BASES network. The Conference will be held in early 2007 in Columbia. Anti-war organizations all over the world are helping with the organization. Lalit is on the International Organizing Committee.
