Stop Press from Ragini Kistnasamy on way to Gaza
30.12.2009
Ragini Kistnasamy and the 100 delegates that the Egyptian Government has allowed to go through the Rafah Crossing into Gaza were on their way to Gaza, when they stopped in order to hold consultations with the organizations in Gaza receiving them. Together, during a telephone contact, they took the decision not to enter Gaza UNLESS all 1,400 delegates are allowed into Gaza by the Egyptian Government. This information was received by phone from Kisna at 11:10 am in Mauritius
