Ram Seegobin on Radio Plus today 8.15a.m.
10.08.2004
Today, 10th August, Ram Seegobin, Lalit member, is due to be interviewed by Radio Plus at 8.15 a.m. The interview is on the subject of the double Government attack on free speech: threatening the private radio stations with new restrictions on free speech, and introducing even more draconian legislation on defamation, prepared by a sub-contractor, M. Robert Ahnee.
