LALIT retrospective 2005: LITERATURE: Lalit member's novel BOY wins prestigious Commonwealth Writers' Prize for Africa
06.01.2006
Lalit member, Lindsey Collen's novel BOY, won the 2005 Commonwealth Writers' Prize for Africa. She is one of the few writers to have won one of the regional prizes twice. In 1995 she won the same prize for THE RAPE OF SITA.
