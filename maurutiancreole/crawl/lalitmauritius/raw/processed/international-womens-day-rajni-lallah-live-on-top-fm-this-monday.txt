International Women's Day: Rajni Lallah live on Top FM this Monday
07.03.2004
Rajni Lallah, spokesperson of Lalit and the Women's Liberation Movement (MLF) will be live on Top FM Radio, this  Monday, 8 March. She will be the guest of Darlmah Naeck in his Hard Talk program on occasion of the International Women's Day. Ragini Kistnasamy spokesperson of the MLF was live on Radio Plus on the occasion of the international women's day. Ragini Kistnasamy is also a Lalit member and was amongst the main the speakers of the first ever celebration of the International Women's Day, in Mauritius in 1978.
