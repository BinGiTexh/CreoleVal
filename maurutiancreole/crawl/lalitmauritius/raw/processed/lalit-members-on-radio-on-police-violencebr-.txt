Lalit members on Radio on Police Violence
26.01.2006
Lalit, known for its long standing campaign against police violence, has been invited in various radio debates and programmes to speak on police violence. Yesterday, Ram Seegobin was on Top FM in the Hardtalk debate. Lindsey Collen was on the Pour ou Contre programme on Radio Plus on Saturday morning and spoke during the debate last Monday two days after Mr Rajesh Ramlogun's death, Alain Ah-Vee was on Top FM Wednesday morning, and Rajni Lallah was in a debate after the Grand Journal on Radio Plus on Tuesday last.
