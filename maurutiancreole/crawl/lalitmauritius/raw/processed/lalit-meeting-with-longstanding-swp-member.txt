LALIT meeting with longstanding SWP member
02.10.2011
A delegation of LALIT members met with Andy Wynne, a longstanding member of the Socialist Workers Party in Britain, on Wednesday 28 September. There was discussion on continued links between the two parties, as well as with groups in African countries. Over a meal, there was discussion on the situation in Libya. Andy Wynne brought a number of books and booklets as well as copies of their newspaper, while LALIT gave him some of our publications.
