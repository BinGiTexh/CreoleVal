Course in Kreol as a Foreign Language
18.10.2006
Ledikasyon pu Travayer has pleasure in inviting people to enrol for a course in "Kreol as a Foreign Language". The course is being given to Non-Mauritians, and all from abroad are invited, whether they be working here, studying or doing research or married to a Mauritian. The ability to speak Kreol has become increasingly important for all visitors who want to know the country. LPT is offering four "enjoyable sessions".They will start on Saturday 28 October, from 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm, and then there will be three Tuesday morning sessions: 31 October, 7 & 14 November 8:30 am to 10:00 pm. Situational and generative methods, designed to give confidence to start to speak Kreol. The fees are Rs1,000 for the entire course of four sessions. Courses will all be held at Ledikasyon pu Travayer Hall, GRNW, Main Road, near bridge. For admission, phone 208 2132, or just arrive on 28 October.
