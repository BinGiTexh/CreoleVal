Kreol version of paper by Dr. Mike Cole on equality in teacher-training
07.09.2008
LALIT has today in its Documents Section published in toto the Mauritian Kreol language version of Dr. Mike Cole's seminal paper on the issues around 'equality' in teacher-training and teaching. He delivered the paper in April, 2008 at the International Conference organized jointly bythe MIE, the University of Brighton and the Association of Researchers in Education and Training. Just click on the Documents Section and you will find the paper.
