Lalit's poster campaign in villages
15.07.2003
LALIT has launched a poster campaign in villages to protest against the adoption of the new Local Government Act by the MSM/MMM regime. Since Sunday 13th July, Lalit's posters can be seen in various villages of the country. The slogan on the poster is "MSM/MMM finn Kraz Konsey Vilaz pu Taxe Vilazwa"  meaning  "The MSM/MMM crushed Village Councils to impose tax on Village dwellers". Various grassroots activists in villages are joining Lalit to conduct this poster campaign.
