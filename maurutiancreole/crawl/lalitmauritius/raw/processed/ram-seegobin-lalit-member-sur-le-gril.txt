Ram Seegobin (LALIT member) "Sur le Gril"
12.09.2003
On Radio One, at 12 midday on Sunday 14th September, LALIT Central Committee member Ram Seegobin has been invited to participate in a radio programme which will deal mainly with the opposition to further negociations at the Cancun WTO Ministerial Conference, and with LALIT's criticism of the WTO itself. This will be the first time that a LALIT member has been invited to participate in a programme on this particular private commercial Radio station, closely linked to the L"EXPRESS daily newspaper.
