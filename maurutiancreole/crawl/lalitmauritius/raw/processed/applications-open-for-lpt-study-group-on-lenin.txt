Applications open for LPT study group on Lenin
25.09.2008
A new study group on some key Lenin texts will be meeting every other Monday evening. The group is open for admissions on Monday 29 September at 7:30 pm at Ledikasyon pu Travayer's building in GRNW. The texts to be studied are Lenine's speech on the State at the Sverdlov University, The State and Revolution, Imperialism: The Highest Stage of Capitalism, Ultraleftism: An infantile disorder, and the April Theses. The cost, including the texts, will be Rs 755 for the entire series of 10 sessions.For details write to lptmail@intnet.mu or telephone 208 2132.
