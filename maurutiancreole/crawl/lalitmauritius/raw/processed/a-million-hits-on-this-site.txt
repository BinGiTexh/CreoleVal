A million hits on this site
20.10.2010
LALIT has had more than a million hits on our web-site, www.lalitmauritius.org since it was first set up. In the first year, 2002, we received 242 hits, and the next year 17,161 and the number of hits has seen a constant progression, as people have found out about the existence of the site. This year alone we have already received over 300,000 hits, which is over 30,000 a month and over one thousand a day.
