Call for Witnesses re Suppression of the Mother Tongue in School
23.09.2009
The association Ledikasyon pu Travayer is at present making a public call for witnesses to come forward to give testimony on personal experiences of having one's language stifled in the school system. If you, or your child, have first-hand experience of your languages being suppressed, banned, sneered at, while at school, please come forward to give evidence. There will be a panel of seven experienced men and women (four Mauritians and three from abroad) who will listen to evidence during the "Hearing", and then make their findings public.If you would like to give evidence, please contact Alain or Lindsey at lptmail@intnet.mu or ring 208 2132.
